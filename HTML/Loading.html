On the Subject of Loading

Thank goodness! We only had a second left.

Let the module load. You can help it along by solving modules at the right time.

Each time another module is solved, the loading bar advances. Add one to the number of currently solved modules and multiply the result by the last digit of the seconds on the bomb's timer. The result of this, modulo 3 gives the minutes added to the loading bar.

Warning: If the bar fills completely before half of the bomb’s modules are solved, the bomb will detonate.
